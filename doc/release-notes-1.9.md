# Approach Note 1.9 — Release Notes

## Song & artist pages
- Song descriptions and artist biographies now expand inline with a **Read more / Read less** link, and fade out gently when collapsed so it's clear there's more to read.
- Added a **Wikipedia** button to the *Learn More* section, alongside Jazz Standards and MusicBrainz.

## Recordings
- Recordings are now organized into cleaner cards grouped by decade, and the **first group opens automatically** so you see recordings right away.
- The **Filter and Sort** controls now sit side by side for quicker access.

## About & launch screen
- Refreshed the **About** screen with a new background image and a cleaner layout.
- Fixed the **launch screen** so it fills the whole screen on iPad instead of showing black bars.

Plus assorted visual polish and spacing refinements throughout.
