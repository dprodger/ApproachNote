YouTube Walk

Bulk-enqueue YouTube match jobs for every recording. Each recording becomes one youtube.match_recording job in research_jobs. Progress is visible on the research dashboard (filter source = youtube).

Controls

Caps recordings, not songs. Leave blank to walk everything.

Quick search

Use the on-disk YouTube search cache where available (cache TTL: 30 days). Skips recordings that already have a YouTube link. Cheap on quota — most jobs cost zero units.

Deep search

Bypass the cache (every search hits the API) and re-evaluate recordings that already have a match. Quota-hungry — at 100 units per search, a full walk can consume a substantial chunk of the daily budget.
